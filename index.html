ABOUT US

Richie Careers Management is an international talent management, booking, and career development agency dedicated to discovering, launching, and representing actors and models across the United Kingdom, United States, Europe, and Kuwait.

Built on diversity, creativity, and authentic collaboration, we work with emerging and established talent in fashion, commercial, beauty, voiceover, theatrical, television, film, and digital media. Our mission is to help actors and models build successful, sustainable careers while creating opportunities in some of the world's most competitive entertainment and fashion markets.

We specialize in talent launching and career development, providing strategic guidance, professional branding, portfolio development, casting support, and direct access to industry opportunities. Through innovative marketing and effective use of social media, we help our talent increase visibility, strengthen their personal brand, and connect with key decision-makers.

Our team takes a hands-on and personalized approach to representation. We create customized talent packages and present them directly to casting directors, production companies, brands, agencies, and clients, ensuring a professional, efficient, and collaborative booking process.

With over two decades of combined industry experience, we understand the evolving demands of the global entertainment and fashion industries. We work closely with both talent and clients to deliver exceptional results through strong communication, transparency, and strategic career planning.

At Richie Careers Management, we proudly represent talent of all genders, sizes, backgrounds, and identities. We believe individuality is a strength, and our goal is to open doors, launch careers, and create opportunities that reflect the diverse future of fashion, film, television, and entertainment worldwide.

Our mission is simple: Discover talent. Launch careers. Create global opportunities.

BECOME A MODEL

To apply for modeling opportunities, please submit your images via our website.

For fashion models, we ask that you submit your measurements along with digitals taken in simple, natural lighting and with minimal makeup.

For those interested in our commercial and print division, there is no need to include digitals. Instead, please provide your headshots, a resume detailing your previous bookings in commercial print or voice-over work, and any additional skills or hobbies that might be relevant.

If your profile aligns with what we’re looking for, one of our agents will be in touch with you directly.

DIGITAL GUIDELINES:

  1. Headshot
  2. Profile
  3. Half body shot
  4. Full length body shot
  5. 3/4 shot straight on
  6. No makeup or retouched
  7. No posing, be natural
  8. White background
  9. Plain tank top & jeans
  10. Good natural lighting

AGENCY STANDARDS

BOOKING CONDITIONS

The following conditions ensure a smooth, professional experience for all parties when booking with our agency.

01

Agency Fees

A 20% agency fee applies to all bookings unless otherwise agreed in writing.

02

Model Rates & Overtime

Rates are based on an eight-hour day. Overtime is billed at time-and-a-half beyond eight hours unless other terms are negotiated.

03

Usage Rights

Usage is limited to what is agreed upon at booking. Any extended or additional usage requires prior written approval.

04

Payment Terms & Late Fees

Payments are due by the agreed date. Late payments will incur a 10% weekly penalty until settled.

05

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations within 24 hours of the call time incur the full fee. Within 48 hours, half fee.

06

Weather Permits

For same-day weather-related cancellations, half fee applies if rescheduled immediately, full fee if not. Early weather cancellations follow standard terms. "Book or release" applies when secondary jobs are ready.

07

Reshoots & Rebookings

If additional shoot days or re-casting are required, new availability and additional fees will apply based on the scope.

08

Travel Policies

Travel exceeding 3 hours one-way or requiring overnight stay will incur additional fees. Clients are responsible for travel costs unless otherwise agreed.

09

Communication Protocol

All communication must go through our agency. Clients may not contact talent directly without prior consent.

By proceeding with bookings, you agree to these terms. We are committed to fostering a professional, supportive, and dignified environment for our models. Your adherence to these conditions helps protect and uphold that standard.
